This project provides a comprehensive financial and operational analysis of ITC Hotels, focusing on revenue performance, occupancy trends, booking behaviors, and cancellation impacts. It is structured across four key areas:

Financial Overview & Revenue Performance – Analyzes total and cumulative revenue across hotels and room categories, tracks month-over-month and week-over-week growth, and evaluates key performance metrics such as Average Daily Rate (ADR) and Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR).

Occupancy & Capacity Analysis – Examines occupancy rates by hotel and room type, identifies seasonal and periodic fluctuations, and explores the relationship between occupancy and revenue performance.

Room Category Performance & Booking Insights – Assesses revenue contributions from different room categories, explores booking patterns including lead time and check-in behavior, and calculates the Average Length of Stay (ALOS).

Cancellations & Lost Revenue Analysis – Tracks cancellation rates and their trends, quantifies lost revenue due to cancellations, and identifies patterns to mitigate future loss.

The project is driven by key KPIs including total revenue, occupancy rate, ALOS, RevPAR, booking lead time, and cancellation metrics, offering actionable insights for strategic decision-making.
